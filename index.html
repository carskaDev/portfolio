My key skills

MASTERMIND

My interest and greatest skill is being innovating and coming up with ideas. World creations, puzzles, character development, rpg battle mechanism - all that and more. I tend to create over the top and complex setups and plot archs, but I also like doing small, simple setups which are aimed towards more casual games. I like playing around with themes such as cosmic horror, morality, ethics, robots and technology. Genres vary from deck building to survival horror shooters and rpgs. I've some knowledge of writing GDD's and I have quite a few of those in various stages of completion.

TEAM LEADER

I care about people and that can be seen in my mentality of leadership. I make sure that the people I work with are in best condition to work with the projects, so I constanty remind and talk about the importance of the mental and physical health. I approach the people I work with with positivity and warmth. In development discussions and during more serious talks I make sure that the feedback is constructive and the discussion ends with a high note, even the most sensitive feedback and be delivered with positivity. Working in small groups as a product owner is something I'm already used to and from which I have more experience. I've managed a game store and worked with multiple projects as a product owner or a team leader.

ALL AROUND WORKER

I'm not a perfect worker by all means, but I an a quick and avid learner. I like to expand my knowledge everytime it's possible and know the project I'm working with thoroughly. That's why I want to dive into all the aspects of the work process. I already have some knowledge about sounds and music (I am a sort of musician myself), level design, game design and coding. Tools and softwares like Unity, GameMaker 2, Logic Pro X, Photoshop, Aseprite and few more essential tools. More about these skills in my CV.
